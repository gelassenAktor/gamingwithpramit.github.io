INFORMATION ABOUT COUNTER STRIKE


Counter-Strike is a series of multiplayer first person shooter video games in which teams of terrorists fight an act of terrorism while anti-terrorists try to prevent it. The series began on Windows in 1999 with the release of the first game, Counter-Strike. You need to have a steam account in order to play Counter Strike.
