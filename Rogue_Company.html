ROGUE COMPANY

INFORMATION ABOUT ROGUE COMPANY

Rogue Company is a free-to-play multiplayer tactical third-person hero shooter video game developed by First Watch Games and published by Hi-Rez Studios. The game was released in open beta on October 1, 2020 for Microsoft Windows via the Epic Games Store, Xbox One, PlayStation 4, Nintendo Switch,[1] with an Xbox Series X/S release following on November 25, 2020,[2] and a PlayStation 5 release on March 30, 2021.[3] The game features full support for cross-platform play and cross-progression.
